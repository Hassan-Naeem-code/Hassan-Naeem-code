Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

What I do not collect

What happens when you visit

The site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel, as the hosting provider, processes standard server access logs (IP address, user-agent, timestamp, requested path) for the purpose of delivering the site and preventing abuse. These logs are governed by Vercel's own privacy policy.

Third-party resources

All assets (icons, fonts, stylesheets, scripts) are self-hosted on this domain. Your browser does not contact any third-party CDN while loading this site.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because I do not collect, store, or process personal data, there is nothing to access, rectify, delete, or port. If you still have a question, you can contact me using the links on the portfolio's contact section.

Security

The site is served over HTTPS, with HSTS preload, strict Content Security Policy, Referrer-Policy, Permissions-Policy, and X-Frame-Options headers to protect against clickjacking and other browser-level attacks.

Changes
