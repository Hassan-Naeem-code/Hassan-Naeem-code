Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

This site (hassan-naeem.vercel.app) is the personal portfolio of Muhammad Hassan Naeem. It is a static website — there is no backend, no database, no user accounts, no forms, no analytics, and no tracking.

What I do not collect

  • Cookies (none are set)
  • Analytics or tracking pixels (none)
  • Personal information submitted through forms (there are no forms)
  • Email addresses, names, or any other identifiers

What happens when you visit

The site is hosted on Vercel. Vercel, as the hosting provider, processes standard server access logs (IP address, user-agent, timestamp, requested path) for the purpose of delivering the site and preventing abuse. These logs are governed by Vercel's own privacy policy.

Third-party resources

This site loads a small stylesheet of icons from unicons.iconscout.com over HTTPS, protected by Subresource Integrity. Your browser makes a request to that CDN which logs standard access information. No other third parties are contacted. Fonts are self-hosted on this domain, so no requests are made to Google Fonts or any other font CDN.

Your rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Because I do not collect, store, or process personal data, there is nothing to access, rectify, delete, or port. If you still have a question, you can contact me using the links on the portfolio's contact section.

Security

The site is served over HTTPS, with HSTS preload, strict Content Security Policy, Referrer-Policy, Permissions-Policy, and X-Frame-Options headers to protect against clickjacking and other browser-level attacks.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
